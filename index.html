ABOUT

EdPlat is a learning platform developed for students and faculties to develop a good educational environment with the support of effective communication facilities. The main idea of EdPlat is to create a stand alone platform that integrates various functionalities such as group discussion, doubt clearance, organizing classes, sharing of study materials, chatting, assignment submissions, grading etc, in order to create a distraction free environment.
